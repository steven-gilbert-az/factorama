Detailed Description

Prior constraint on camera orientation.

Applies a prior constraint to the rotation component of a PoseVariable. Useful for incorporating IMU measurements or known orientations.

double orientation_sigma = 0.1;
auto pose_orientation_prior = std::make_shared<PoseOrientationPriorFactor>(
factor_id++, camera_pose, Eigen::Vector3d::Zero(), orientation_sigma);
