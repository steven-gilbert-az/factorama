Detailed Description

6-DOF camera pose variable with SE(3) parameterization

Represents camera poses as 6-element vectors: [tx, ty, tz, rx, ry, rz] where translation is in world frame and rotation uses rodrigues vector (axis * angle) representation. The pose transforms points from world to camera frame.

Eigen::Matrix<double, 6, 1> pose_vec = Eigen::Matrix<double, 6, 1>::Zero();
auto camera_pose = std::make_shared<PoseVariable>(1, pose_vec);
