Detailed Description

Orientation-only relative constraint between pose variables.

Constrains the rotation difference between two poses to match a calibration rotation variable. Only affects the rotation components of poses, ignoring translation. Commonly used for camera-IMU calibration where relative orientation is known or estimated.

auto orientation_constraint = std::make_shared<PoseOrientationBetweenFactor>(
factor_id++, pose1, pose2, calibration_rotation, angle_sigma);
